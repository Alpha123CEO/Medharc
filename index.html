Our Services

       

We offer targeted services for healthcare organizations, payers, and legal teams that need reliable compliance and audit capabilities.

     
   
               

Release of Information (ROI)

               

We provide secure, accurate, and HIPAA-compliant ROI services for hospitals, clinics, law firms, and insurance companies. Secure handling of protected health information (PHI) with documented workflows and audit trails. We support disclosures for treatment, legal requests, and payer inquiries.

               

Medical Billing Audits

               

Full-scope billing audits to identify coding errors, missed charges, and revenue leakage. Deliverables include a findings report, estimated recoverable amounts, and corrective action recommendations. This helps reduce denials and improve financial performance.

               

Healthcare Compliance & Regulatory Audits

               

Regulatory readiness assessments and gap analyses focused on **HIPAA**, state-specific privacy laws, and payer contract compliance. Remediation plans are prioritized by risk and effort, including documentation and policy verification.

               

Medical Coding Review

               

Certified experts review **ICD-10, CPT, HCPCS, and DRG** assignments to ensure coding accuracy and reduce financial or legal risks. We assist clinics and hospitals in maintaining up-to-date and compliant coding practices.

Consulting & Advisory Services

We provide tailored advisory support for healthcare facilities needing guidance in operational workflow, ROI process optimization, Fraud Waste & Abuse (FWA) review, and documentation accuracy.

  • Operational workflow improvement
  • ROI process optimization
  • Fraud waste & abuse (FWA) review
  • EHR process alignment
  • Strategic guidance for long-term compliance programs.

Audit & Case Review Support

MEDHARC, LLC supports attorneys, insurance companies, and healthcare organizations with **independent case reviews**, chart audits, and documentation summaries for litigation or internal investigation.

Medical Billing Services

We offer end-to-end billing solutions including claim submission, insurance follow-up, denial management, and payment posting. Our goal is to help clients improve reimbursement accuracy and maintain clean claims.

Service Packages (Examples)

  • **Baseline Audit** — one-time assessment + prioritized report.
  • **Recovery Audit** — focused billing review with recovery assistance.
  • **Managed Compliance** — ongoing audits, training, and dashboarding.

Custom pricing is available based on volume, complexity, and scope. Contact us for a tailored proposal.
