About MEDHARC, LLC

We are a healthcare compliance and auditing firm specializing in Release of Information, medical billing audits, and regulatory review services. We help healthcare organizations stay compliant, reduce errors, and improve operational accuracy through precise and ethical auditing practices. Our team brings expertise in health informatics, HIPAA compliance, and clinical administration to deliver trusted, high-quality results. We are committed to maintaining integrity, confidentiality, and accuracy in every step of the audit and information-release process. With a focus on innovation and client-centric solutions, We ensures reliable support for providers, insurance companies, and legal entities.

Our Story

MEDHARC, LLC began with a vision to bring accuracy, transparency, and compliance to healthcare documentation and auditing. Founded by a Health Informatics professional with expertise in HIPAA compliance, clinical operations, and healthcare analytics, the company was built to solve real challenges in medical data management. We noticed that many healthcare organizations struggled with proper ROI handling, billing accuracy, and regulatory requirements—so we created a service that ensures precision at every step. Our commitment is simple: deliver trustworthy, efficient, and compliant solutions that empower providers and organizations to focus on patient care. Today, MEDHARC continues to grow as a trusted partner for audits, information release services, and healthcare compliance consulting. MEDHARC was founded to help healthcare organizations reduce risk, recover revenue, and standardize compliant workflows. We blend technical health informatics experience with practical auditing methods so clients receive outcomes they can act on immediately.
